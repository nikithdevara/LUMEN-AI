# ✨ LUMEN AI

## Illuminate. Understand. Act.

**LUMEN AI** is an AI-powered interactive awareness and education platform that transforms awareness into meaningful understanding and action through personalized storytelling, reflection, and AI-driven learning experiences.

The platform uses Generative AI to create role-based scenarios where users can explore real-world situations, make decisions, understand their impact, reflect on lessons, and receive personalized recommendations.

---

## 🌍 About LUMEN AI

Many people understand human trafficking only through stereotypes or extreme examples, which creates gaps in awareness and prevention.

LUMEN AI addresses this challenge by providing a safe, empathetic, and educational AI experience that helps people understand:

• Hidden warning signs  
• Vulnerability and manipulation patterns  
• Responsible responses  
• Prevention strategies  
• Community actions  

The platform follows the learning journey:

**Awareness → Understanding → Reflection → Action**

---

# 🚀 Core Features

## 🤖 AI-Powered Interactive Storytelling

LUMEN AI creates personalized learning experiences based on the user's role.

Supported user roles:

🎓 Student  
👨‍👩‍👧 Parent  
🏨 Hotel Staff  
🤝 Volunteer  

Each experience adapts to the user's perspective and provides relevant scenarios.

---

## 🎭 Interactive Decision-Based Learning

Users explore realistic situations and make decisions.

The AI explains:

• Why a choice matters  
• Important awareness indicators  
• Safer approaches  
• Responsible actions  

The goal is not to judge users, but to help them learn through experience.

---

## 🧠 AI Reflection Engine

After completing a scenario, users reflect on their learning.

The AI helps summarize:

• Key lessons learned  
• Important observations  
• Personal insights  
• Recommended actions  

---

## 📚 Knowledge Check

A short AI-powered assessment reinforces learning.

Features:

• Interactive questions  
• Instant explanations  
• Learning score  
• Key takeaways  

---

## 🎯 Personalized Recommendations

Based on:

• User role  
• Story decisions  
• Reflection responses  
• Learning progress  

LUMEN AI provides personalized:

• Awareness resources  
• Educational materials  
• Safety guidance  
• Action recommendations
